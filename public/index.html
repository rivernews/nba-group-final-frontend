WEIGHTS & HEIGHTS OF NBA PLAYERS

SI649 Group Project | Mu He, Shaung Cheng, Huiyi Huang, Zhiyi Ma, Xi Li

In the last decades, there is a trend in NBA that players become more versatile (Center and Power Forward like Draymond Green become more agile and good at dribbling and assisting, Point Guard like Derrick Rose and Russell Westbrook become stronger and good at physical confrontation). Influenced by this trend, players in the league have generally become taller and stronger over time. To identify this assumption, we conducted several visualizations as above shows.
